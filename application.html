Application Process

Ready to take the next step? Learn about our application process and requirements.

How to Apply

1

Initial Contact & Pre-Screening

Contact us by phone or through our online inquiry system. We'll conduct a brief pre-screening to ensure our community is a good fit for your goals and current situation.

What we'll discuss: Your goals, timeline, current situation, and basic eligibility requirements.

2

Complete Application

Fill out our comprehensive application through our secure online portal via SobrietyHub. This includes personal information, references, and background details.

Application Portal: Secure, confidential application process through our partner platform.

3

Background Verification

We conduct thorough but respectful background checks including criminal history, employment verification, and reference checks to ensure community safety.

Timeline: Background checks typically take 3-5 business days to complete.

4

Interview & House Tour

Meet with our management team for a personal interview and tour of available housing. This helps ensure mutual compatibility and answers any questions you may have.

What to expect: 30-45 minute interview, house tour, and discussion of house guidelines.

5

Approval & Move-In

Upon approval, we'll provide a move-in date and complete the necessary paperwork. First month's rent and security deposit are due before move-in.

Move-in ready: Most approved applicants can move in within 1-2 weeks.

Application Requirements

Basic Eligibility

  • ✓ 18 years of age or older
  • ✓ Commitment to clean and sober lifestyle
  • ✓ Stable source of income or benefits
  • ✓ Willingness to follow house guidelines
  • ✓ Clear criminal background (case by case)

Required Documents

  • ✓ Government-issued photo ID
  • ✓ Proof of income or benefits
  • ✓ Emergency contact information
  • ✓ Previous housing references
  • ✓ Medical clearance (if applicable)
