How to Apply

Our streamlined application process ensures we find the right fit for our transitional housing community. Each step is designed to help us understand your needs and goals.

1

Initial Contact

Reach out via phone or email to express interest

2

Program Selection

Choose the program that best fits your situation and needs

3

Application Form

Complete detailed application with program-specific questions

4

Review Process

Background check, references, and program-specific evaluation

5

Move-In

Approval, payment processing, and program orientation

Application Requirements

General Requirements

  • ✓ Commitment to community guidelines and structure
  • ✓ Active participation in assigned program
  • ✓ Employment or active job search/training
  • ✓ Clean background check (case-by-case review)
  • ✓ Positive references from support system

Documents Needed

  • • Valid government-issued ID
  • • Proof of income or benefits
  • • Emergency contact information
  • • Three personal/professional references
  • • Program-specific documentation (if applicable)
