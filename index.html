Living Standards & Guidelines

Our structured environment promotes accountability, personal growth, and lasting recovery through clear expectations and supportive community living.

Sobriety Requirements

  • • Zero tolerance for alcohol and illegal substances
  • • Random testing to ensure compliance
  • • Active participation in recovery programs
  • • Immediate termination for violations

Daily Responsibilities

  • • Maintain employment or active job search
  • • Keep personal living areas clean
  • • Participate in house duties and chores
  • • Respect quiet hours and house rules
