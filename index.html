About Us

Building bridges to brighter futures through community, accountability, and unwavering support

Our Mission

Forward Horizon exists to provide safe, structured transitional housing and comprehensive support services for veterans, individuals in recovery, and returning citizens. We believe that everyone deserves a second chance and the opportunity to rebuild their lives with dignity, purpose, and community support.

Safe, Welcoming Community

Our Story

Forward Horizon was founded on the belief that transition periods—whether from military service, addiction, or incarceration—are critical moments that require specialized support and understanding.

Our founders recognized that traditional approaches often failed to address the unique challenges faced by these populations. Through years of community work and personal experience, Forward Horizon emerged as a comprehensive solution that addresses not just housing, but the holistic needs of individuals in transition.

Today, we continue to evolve our programs based on the needs of our residents and the lessons learned from our community's collective journey toward healing and growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our housing programs, services, and application process.

What's the application process for housing?

Our streamlined application process includes completing our online form, participating in a brief interview, and providing basic documentation. We work with you every step of the way to ensure a smooth transition into our community.

How long can residents stay in transitional housing?

Our transitional housing programs typically range from 6 months to 2 years, depending on individual progress and program type. We focus on helping you achieve sustainable independence at your own pace.

What support services are included?

Our comprehensive services include 24/7 on-site support staff, individual and group counseling, case management, life skills training, job placement assistance, transportation help, and medical referrals.

What are the house rules and expectations?

Our community guidelines promote safety, respect, and recovery. This includes maintaining sobriety, participating in required programs, contributing to household responsibilities, and treating all community members with dignity.

Is there a cost for housing services?

Our programs are designed to be affordable and accessible. Costs vary by program, and we work with residents to develop payment plans. Many services are covered through partnerships with local agencies and organizations.

What makes Forward Horizon different from other programs?

We focus on individualized care, community building, and long-term success. Our experienced staff, peer support model, and comprehensive wrap-around services create an environment where healing and growth naturally occur.

Do you accept veterans with PTSD or other conditions?

Absolutely. We specialize in serving veterans with various conditions including PTSD, TBI, and substance use disorders. Our staff is trained in trauma-informed care and veteran-specific challenges and strengths.

Can I bring my belongings and personal items?

Yes, residents are encouraged to bring personal belongings that help make their space feel like home. We provide secure storage and work with you to ensure your important items are safely kept during your stay.

What happens if I relapse or have a setback?

Recovery is a journey with ups and downs. We provide compassionate support during difficult times, connecting you with appropriate resources and working together to get back on track. Your success is our priority.

How do you help with employment and job training?

We offer comprehensive employment support including resume building, interview preparation, job search assistance, skills training, and connections with local employers. Our goal is to help you achieve financial independence.
