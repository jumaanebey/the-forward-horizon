## Detailed Test Configurations

### Veterans Page Tests

#### Test 1: Headline Urgency vs. Benefits
**Test Name:** `veterans_headline_test`
**Traffic Split:** 50/50
**Duration:** 4 weeks
**Primary Metric:** Form completion rate

**Variant A (Control):** 
"Secure, Supportive Housing for Veterans"

**Variant B (Test):** 
"Emergency Veterans Housing Available Now"

**Hypothesis:** Urgency-focused headlines will increase conversion rates for veterans needing immediate housing.

**Success Criteria:** 15% improvement in conversion rate

## Test Implementation Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation Tests (Weeks 1-4)
- Start with headline and primary CTA tests
- Focus on highest-traffic pages first
- Establish baseline metrics

### Phase 2: Refinement Tests (Weeks 5-8)  
- Test social proof and trust elements
- Optimize form placement and design
- Test secondary messaging elements

### Phase 3: Advanced Tests (Weeks 9-12)
- Test page layout variations
- Test different lead magnets
- Test pricing presentation (if applicable)

## Statistical Requirements

### Minimum Sample Sizes:
- **Veterans Page:** 1,000 visitors per variant (2,000 total)
- **Recovery Page:** 1,200 visitors per variant (2,400 total)
- **Reentry Page:** 800 visitors per variant (1,600 total)

### Confidence Level: 95%
### Statistical Power: 80%
### Minimum Detectable Effect: 15% relative improvement

### Test Duration Guidelines:
- **Minimum:** 1 week (to account for day-of-week effects)
- **Maximum:** 4 weeks (to avoid seasonal effects)
- **Typical:** 2-3 weeks for statistical significance

## Measurement and Analysis

### Primary Metrics:
1. **Form Completion Rate** - % of visitors who complete the lead form
2. **Click-Through Rate** - % of visitors who click the primary CTA
3. **Time on Page** - Average engagement time
4. **Bounce Rate** - % of visitors who leave immediately

### Secondary Metrics:
1. **Form Start Rate** - % who begin filling out the form
2. **Phone Call Clicks** - % who click phone number
3. **Main Website CTR** - % who click through to theforwardhorizon.com
4. **Social Share Rate** - % who share the page

### Segment Analysis:
- **Traffic Source** (Google Ads, Facebook, Organic, etc.)
- **Device Type** (Mobile, Desktop, Tablet)
- **Geographic Location**
- **Time of Day/Day of Week**
- **New vs. Returning Visitors**

## Risk Management

### Potential Risks:
1. **Traffic Fluctuations:** Seasonal or external factors affecting results
2. **Technical Issues:** Tracking problems or page loading issues  
3. **External Events:** News or events affecting target audiences
4. **Sample Contamination:** Same user seeing multiple variants

### Mitigation Strategies:
1. **Minimum Test Duration:** Ensure tests run long enough for valid results
2. **Quality Monitoring:** Daily checks on tracking and performance
3. **External Factor Documentation:** Note any significant events during tests
4. **Proper Segmentation:** Ensure clean user assignment to variants

### Rollback Plan:
If any test variant shows significantly worse performance:
1. Pause test immediately
2. Redirect all traffic to control variant
3. Analyze data to understand the decline
4. Revise hypothesis and test design
5. Resume testing with improved variant
