House Rules & Guidelines

Creating a safe, supportive, and structured living environment for all residents

The Forward Horizon is committed to providing a safe, supportive, and structured living environment. These house rules are designed to ensure the well-being of all residents and maintain a positive community atmosphere that promotes personal growth and recovery.

Safety & Security

  • • All doors must remain locked at all times for security purposes
  • • Residents must check in with house management upon arrival and departure
  • • No unauthorized visitors or guests without prior approval
  • • Emergency contact information must be kept current at all times

Sobriety Standards

  • • Zero tolerance policy for alcohol and illegal substances on premises
  • • Random testing may be conducted to ensure compliance
  • • Residents must maintain active participation in recovery programs
  • • Any violation results in immediate termination of housing agreement

Daily Living Guidelines

Cleanliness & Maintenance

  • • Keep personal living areas clean and organized
  • • Participate in scheduled house cleaning duties
  • • Report maintenance issues promptly
  • • Respect shared common areas

Quiet Hours & Respect

  • • Quiet hours: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM daily
  • • Respect all residents and staff
  • • No loud music or disturbances
  • • Communicate conflicts respectfully

Financial Responsibilities

  • • Rent payments are due between the 1st and 4th of each month
  • • Late fees apply after the 5th of the month
  • • Move-in fee must be paid prior to occupancy
  • • Residents are responsible for personal property insurance

Employment & Personal Development

  • • Residents must maintain employment or be actively seeking employment
  • • Participation in educational or vocational programs encouraged
  • • Regular meetings with house management to discuss progress
  • • Goal-setting and accountability partnerships supported

Consequences & Support

Progressive Intervention

We believe in supporting residents through challenges. Minor violations result in counseling and additional support. Serious violations may result in probation or termination of housing agreement.

Zero Tolerance

Substance use, violence, theft, or illegal activities result in immediate termination of housing agreement with no refund of fees.
