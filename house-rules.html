Community Guidelines & Rules

Creating a safe, supportive, and structured living environment for all residents

Our Community Commitment

Forward Horizon is committed to providing a safe, supportive, and structured living environment for veterans, recovering individuals, and returning citizens. These community guidelines are designed to ensure the well-being of all residents and maintain a positive atmosphere that promotes personal growth, accountability, and successful transition to independent living.

Veterans

Honor, respect, and brotherhood

🌱

Recovery

Sobriety, accountability, and growth

🔄

Reentry

Fresh starts and community integration

Safety & Security

🔒

Building Security

All exterior doors must remain locked at all times. Residents are responsible for ensuring doors are secured behind them when entering or leaving the building.

📋

Check-In/Check-Out

All residents must check in with community management upon arrival and departure. Overnight absences require prior approval from staff.

👥

Visitors & Guests

Visitors are not permitted in residential areas to maintain privacy and safety. Common area visits must be pre-approved and supervised by staff.

📱

Emergency Contacts

Emergency contact information must be kept current and accessible to staff at all times.

Program-Specific Guidelines

🇺🇸 Veterans Program Guidelines

  • • Active participation in VA programs and appointments as applicable
  • • Respect for military values: honor, integrity, and service
  • • Participation in veteran peer support groups and activities
  • • Utilization of available veteran resources and benefits

🌱 Recovery Program Guidelines

  • • Zero tolerance policy for alcohol and illegal substances
  • • Mandatory participation in recovery meetings (AA/NA/SMART Recovery)
  • • Random drug and alcohol testing as part of accountability
  • • Working with a sponsor or recovery mentor
  • • Participation in house recovery activities and education

🔄 Reentry Program Guidelines

  • • Compliance with all parole/probation requirements
  • • Active participation in reentry case management services
  • • Attendance at required court dates and legal appointments
  • • Participation in job training and placement programs
  • • Engagement in community service and reintegration activities

Daily Living Guidelines

Cleanliness & Maintenance

  • ✓ Keep personal living areas clean and organized
  • ✓ Participate in scheduled house cleaning duties
  • ✓ Report maintenance issues promptly to staff
  • ✓ Respect and maintain shared common areas
  • ✓ Follow kitchen cleanup procedures after use

Community Respect

  • ✓ Quiet hours: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM daily
  • ✓ Treat all residents and staff with respect
  • ✓ No loud music, television, or disturbances
  • ✓ Address conflicts through proper channels
  • ✓ Respect privacy and personal boundaries

Financial Responsibilities

Payment Requirements

  • 💰 Monthly rent of $1,200 due between 1st-4th of each month
  • 💰 Late fees apply after the 5th of the month
  • 💰 Accepted payment methods: bank transfer, certified check, money order
  • 💰 No cash payments accepted for security purposes

Financial Support

  • 🤝 VA housing benefits accepted for qualified veterans
  • 🤝 Payment plans available for approved applicants
  • 🤝 Sliding scale options based on income verification
  • 🤝 Personal property insurance is resident's responsibility

Employment & Personal Development

Work & Education Requirements

  • 📈 Maintain employment or actively participate in job search activities
  • 📈 Engage in educational or vocational training programs when applicable
  • 📈 Attend regular meetings with case management or program coordinators
  • 📈 Participate in goal-setting and progress review sessions
  • 📈 Engage in community service and volunteer opportunities

Support Available

Forward Horizon provides job placement assistance, resume building support, interview preparation, and connections to local employment resources. We work with each resident to develop individualized employment and education plans based on their goals and program requirements.

Technology & Personal Items

Approved Items

  • ✓ Personal cell phones and tablets
  • ✓ Laptops for work and education
  • ✓ Personal clothing and hygiene items
  • ✓ Reasonable personal decorations
  • ✓ Work tools and equipment (with approval)

Prohibited Items

  • ✗ Weapons of any kind
  • ✗ Alcohol and illegal substances
  • ✗ Large appliances without permission
  • ✗ Candles and open flame items
  • ✗ Pets without prior approval

Progressive Support & Accountability

Our Approach

Forward Horizon believes in supporting residents through challenges while maintaining clear boundaries and accountability. We use a progressive intervention model that emphasizes growth, learning, and second chances whenever possible.

Minor Violations

Examples: Late check-in, minor cleanliness issues, noise complaints

  • • Verbal warning and coaching
  • • Additional support and resources
  • • Skill-building opportunities
  • • Increased check-ins with staff

Serious Violations

Examples: Repeated rule violations, program non-compliance

  • • Written warning and action plan
  • • Probationary period with conditions
  • • Mandatory counseling or support groups
  • • Possible temporary program modification

Zero Tolerance Policy

The following actions result in immediate termination of housing agreement:

  • • Use or possession of alcohol or illegal substances
  • • Violence, threats, or intimidation toward others
  • • Theft or destruction of property
  • • Bringing weapons onto the premises
  • • Illegal activities on or around the property

Appeals & Grievance Process

Residents have the right to appeal disciplinary actions and file grievances about any aspect of their housing experience. We are committed to fair and transparent processes.

1️⃣

Submit Appeal

File written appeal within 72 hours of disciplinary action

2️⃣

Review Process

Management review with resident input and advocacy

3️⃣

Final Decision

Written decision within 5 business days
