Community Manager

Full-time Position | Transitional Housing

Position Overview

We are seeking a dedicated Community Manager to oversee daily operations of our transitional housing facility. This role combines administrative responsibilities with hands-on resident support, creating a safe and supportive environment for veterans, recovering individuals, and returning citizens.

Key Responsibilities

Resident Support
  • • Conduct regular check-ins with residents
  • • Facilitate house meetings and program activities
  • • Provide crisis intervention when needed
  • • Connect residents with community resources
  • • Monitor compliance with community guidelines
  • • Support program-specific initiatives
Administrative Duties
  • • Manage resident intake and orientation
  • • Maintain accurate resident records
  • • Coordinate with healthcare and service providers
  • • Handle financial transactions and rent collection
  • • Prepare monthly reports for management
  • • Coordinate with VA, parole officers, and case managers

Required Qualifications

  • ✓ Bachelor's degree in Social Work, Psychology, or related field preferred
  • ✓ 2+ years experience in transitional housing, social services, or residential care
  • ✓ Understanding of veteran services, recovery principles, or re-entry support
  • ✓ Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • ✓ Crisis intervention training or willingness to obtain
  • ✓ Ability to work flexible hours including evenings and weekends

Preferred Qualifications

  • • Certified Addiction Counselor (CAC) or similar certification
  • • Military service or veteran support experience
  • • Personal experience in recovery (minimum 2 years sobriety)
  • • Criminal justice or re-entry support background
  • • Bilingual capabilities (English/Spanish)
  • • Experience with property management software
  • • First Aid/CPR certification

Compensation & Benefits

Competitive Hourly Rate

$24 - $28 per hour based on experience

Comprehensive Benefits
  • • Health insurance
  • • Paid time off
  • • Professional development
  • • Retirement plan

Work Environment

This position is based at our transitional housing facility and requires a compassionate, patient, and professional individual who can maintain appropriate boundaries while providing support. The role involves both administrative office work and direct interaction with residents from diverse backgrounds in a structured living environment.
